Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • Usage data (game progress, session duration) stored locally on your device
  • Advertising identifiers for ad personalization (via Google AdMob)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the application experience
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app performance

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements. Google Privacy Policy

Data Retention

Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device and are deleted when you uninstall the app. We do not retain your data on our servers.

Children's Privacy

Our application is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

You have the right to opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings. On iOS, go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking. On Android, go to Settings > Google > Ads.
