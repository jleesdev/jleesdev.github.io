Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Thank you for using On The Line. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and disclosed when you use the application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or address.

However, the app may collect certain information automatically through third-party services used within the app.

Advertising Data

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information to provide and improve advertising services, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as the advertising identifier / IDFA)
  • Information about advertisements shown
  • App interaction related to ads

This data may be used to provide personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your device settings and permissions.

App Diagnostics and Performance Data

We may collect anonymous technical information to improve the app's stability and performance, including:

  • Crash reports
  • Performance data
  • Device type and operating system version

This information is not used to identify individual users.

App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS devices, the app may request permission to track your activity across apps and websites owned by other companies using Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework.

If you choose not to allow tracking, the app will still function normally, but ads may be less relevant.

Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

These services include:

  • Google AdMob

For more information, please review Google's privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will promptly remove such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us
