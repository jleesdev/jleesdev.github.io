Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my progress?
Your progress is saved automatically as you play. No manual saving is required.
The game is not loading. What should I do?
Try closing and reopening the app. If the problem persists, try reinstalling the app. Your progress is saved to your device.
How do I remove ads?
Currently the game is free to play and supported by ads. We appreciate your support!
